Use the Input Form to add your class schedule. Select the day, time, course name, type, and room number, then click 'Add'.

Use the Exam Schedule Form to add your exam details. Enter the date, time, and course name, then click 'Add Exam'.

Click on Exam List to view all your scheduled exams. You can also delete any exam from the list.
